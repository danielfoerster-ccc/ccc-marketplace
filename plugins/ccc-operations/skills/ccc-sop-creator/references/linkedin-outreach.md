## Why LinkedIn for CCC

- Posts shown to your existing network → warm audience from day 1
- Every connection who sees AI content thinks "we need to do something about that"
- Intent-based DM outreach has 3–5x higher reply rate than cold DMs
- CCC's ICP (DACH marketing agency owners) is heavily active on LinkedIn

---

## Content Strategy: 3 Post Types

### Type 1: Learn & Share
Post everything you learn while building client solutions or exploring AI tools.
Even basic insights are ahead of 99% of your network. You don't need to be an expert — just one step ahead.

### Type 2: Show Your Work
Share solutions you're building for clients. Even small demos work. Agency owners see problems they can now fix.

### Type 3: Personal Stories
Vulnerability builds trust. Personal brands convert because trust is pre-established.

**Frequency:** 3 posts/week minimum. Volume > quality in early phase.

---

## The Handraiser Post

A "soft CTA" post that asks your network to self-identify as interested.
- Not a sales post. A curiosity/value post that ends with: "If you run a marketing agency and are curious what AI could do for you, drop a comment or DM me."
- Goal: surface warm inbound from existing network
- Timing: Week 1–2 of Phase 1

---

## Intent-Based Outreach (NOT cold DMs)

**The principle:** Only DM people who have already engaged with your content (likes, comments, connection requests). Response rates are 3–5x higher than cold DMs.

**The workflow:**

### Step 1: Pull Engagers
Use [Trigify](https://www.trigify.io/) to scrape everyone who engages with your posts.
Or build n8n flow with [Unipile](https://www.unipile.com/) API.

### Step 2: Filter to ICP
Do NOT message everyone. Filter out:
- Non-decision-makers
- Non-DACH geography
- Non-agency profiles
Trigify has filtering logic built in. Or add it manually in n8n.

### Step 3: Test Manually First
Send DMs manually before automating. 10–20 messages. See what gets replies. Fix the message before scaling.

### Step 4: Scale with HeyReach
Once you know what works, use [HeyReach](https://heyreach.io/) to scale outreach.

## The DM Sequence

**Connection request:**
- Goal: connect, NOT pitch
- Best: no message at all
- If adding a message: reference the post they engaged with. Keep it to 1 sentence.

**Message 1 (after connecting):**
- Still NO pitch
- Open with a question to spark conversation
- Example: "Habt ihr bei [Agency] schon was mit KI-Automatisierung gemacht?"
- Goal: start a conversation, not convert

**Message 2 (if no reply after 5–7 days):**
- Soft pitch: introduce what CCC does, reference a specific pain point
- Keep it short (3–4 sentences max)
- Example: "Falls nicht — ich helfe Marketing-Agenturen dabei, operative Aufgaben mit KI zu automatisieren, damit das Team mehr Zeit für Strategie hat. Wenn das relevant klingt, können wir gerne kurz telefonieren."

**Golden rule:** The goal is a conversation, not a conversion.
