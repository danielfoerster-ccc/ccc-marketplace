## When to Use Cold Email

Cold email works best in Phase 3 — when you are clear on:
- Exact ICP (e.g., DACH marketing agencies, 5–30 employees)
- Specific pain point you solve
- Quantifiable outcome you deliver

Don't start cold email in Phase 1 (network focus) unless you have a strong offer and can segment well.

---

## Infrastructure Setup

### Mailbox Setup (Zapmail recommended)
- Use a **domain variant** for cold email (e.g., `daniel@claudecowork.agency` not your main domain)
- Protects your primary domain's deliverability
- Set up DKIM, SPF, and DMARC for all sending domains
- Warm up new mailboxes for 2–3 weeks before live campaigns
- Use [Zapmail](https://zapmail.app/) to automate mailbox creation and warm-up

### Sending Tool Options
- [Instantly](https://instantly.ai/) — most popular, strong deliverability
- [Lemlist](https://lemlist.com/) — good for personalization at scale
- [Apollo](https://apollo.io/) — combined lead sourcing + outreach

## The Offer Formula

Every cold email is built around a clear offer. Use this structure:

**Niche + Quantifiable End Result + Mechanism + Timeline + Risk Reversal**

Example for CCC:
> "Wir helfen DACH-Marketing-Agenturen mit 5–30 Mitarbeitern, 8–12 Stunden operative Arbeit pro Woche zu automatisieren — in 2 Wochen implementiert, oder du zahlst nichts."

Components:
- **Niche:** DACH marketing agencies, 5–30 employees
- **Result:** Specific number + outcome (8–12 hours/week saved)
- **Mechanism:** AI automation workflows (Claude Cowork)
- **Timeline:** 2 weeks implementation
- **Risk reversal:** Pay only when it works

## Building the Lead List

### Sources
- [Apollo.io](https://apollo.io/) — filter by: industry = marketing agency, employees = 5–30, location = DE/AT/CH
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator — export lists
- Manual research from LinkedIn searches

### Filtering for CCC
Filter leads by:
- Industry: Marketing, Advertising, PR agencies
- Size: 5–30 employees
- Location: DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)
- Title: Founder, CEO, Managing Director, Inhaber
- Remove: freelancers, solo operators, corporations >100 employees

### Enrichment
Add personalization data per lead:
- Company size and recent activity
- Job postings (hiring signals)
- LinkedIn content themes (relevance triggers)

## Email Copy Framework

### Structure
```
Subject: [Curiosity or Specificity — not "Quick question"]
Preview text: [Lead with "why you, why now" — researched hook]

[Personalized first line — show you researched them]

[Pain point + solution in 2 sentences]

[Social proof or demo reference, if available]

[CTA: direct ask for a 20-minute call, with value framing]
```

### Three Campaign Angles

**1. Problem Identification**
Scrape data to find a specific problem, then reference it:
> "Ich habe gesehen, dass ihr gerade 2 Content-Stellen ausschreibt — ich habe für eine ähnliche Agentur ein System gebaut, das den Content-Output verdreifacht hat, ohne mehr Headcount."

**2. Trigger-Based**
React to a recent event (job posting, award, press mention):
> "Glückwunsch zum Pitch-Gewinn bei [Client] — normalerweise steigt mit neuen Projekten die operative Last. Falls Interesse besteht, wie andere Agenturen damit umgehen: gerne 20 Min."

**3. Stacked Targeting**
Only reach out when multiple signals align:
- 5–30 employees AND hiring for ops/content roles AND no AI tools visible on website

### CTA Formula
Not: "Would you want to hop on a call?"
Yes: "Wenn ich euch zeigen kann, wie ihr [specific outcome] in 2 Wochen erreicht — wäre ein 20-Minuten-Call das wert?"

### Preview Text Rule
Put the "why you, why now" hook in the first line — it shows in the email preview. This single change lifts reply rates more than any other copy change.

## Campaign Setup

1. Create campaign in Instantly/Lemlist with 3–5 email touchpoints
2. Touchpoint spacing: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 21
3. Each follow-up adds value (new angle, reference a different pain point)
4. Stop sequence on reply — don't keep emailing someone who responded
5. A/B test: 2 subject lines per campaign minimum

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Deliverability rate | ≥ 95% |
| Open rate | ≥ 40% |
| Reply rate | ≥ 8% (well-segmented list) |
| Positive reply rate | ≥ 3% |
| Discovery calls booked / 100 emails | ≥ 3 |
| Daily send volume | 50–200 emails/day per mailbox |
