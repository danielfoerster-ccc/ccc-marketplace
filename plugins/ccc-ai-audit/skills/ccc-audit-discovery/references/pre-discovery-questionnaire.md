# Pre-Discovery Questionnaire — Template

Send this to the client 2-3 days before the first discovery call. Customize the intro, industry-specific questions, and sign-off for each client.

**Purpose:** Gather baseline context so the discovery call focuses on depth, not basics.
**Client time to complete:** 10-15 minutes.
**Effect:** Reduces discovery call time by 40-60%.

---

## Template

```
Hi [CLIENT NAME],

Thanks for scheduling time to explore automation opportunities! To make our call
as productive as possible, please answer these quick questions. This should take
about 10-15 minutes.

Your responses will help me come prepared with specific ideas tailored to your business.

---

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

1. Company name and industry:
   [Your answer]

2. Number of employees:
   [Your answer]

3. Primary products/services:
   [Your answer]

4. Annual revenue range (optional):
   [ ] Under $1M
   [ ] $1M - $5M
   [ ] $5M - $25M
   [ ] $25M - $100M
   [ ] Over $100M
   [ ] Prefer not to say

---

TECHNOLOGY STACK

5. List all software tools currently used:
   (Examples: Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Asana, QuickBooks, etc.)

   [Your answer]

6. Which tools don't integrate with each other?
   (Example: "Our CRM doesn't talk to our project management system")

   [Your answer]

7. What manual work happens between tools?
   (Example: "We copy customer data from Stripe into our Google Sheet every week")

   [Your answer]

---

PROCESS PRIORITIES

8. What 3 tasks take the most time each week?

   Task 1: [Your answer]
   Task 2: [Your answer]
   Task 3: [Your answer]

9. Where do errors happen most often?
   (Example: "We forget to follow up with leads about 30% of the time")

   [Your answer]

10. What would you automate if it were easy?
    (Don't worry if you don't know how — just describe what you wish happened automatically)

    [Your answer]

---

GOALS AND CONSTRAINTS

11. What business goals matter most in 2026?
    [ ] Increase revenue
    [ ] Reduce operational costs
    [ ] Improve customer experience
    [ ] Scale team without hiring
    [ ] Reduce errors and improve quality
    [ ] Other: [specify]

12. What's your budget range for automation improvements?
    [ ] Under $5,000
    [ ] $5,000 - $15,000
    [ ] $15,000 - $50,000
    [ ] Over $50,000
    [ ] Haven't determined yet

13. What's your timeline for improvements?
    [ ] Immediate (within 30 days)
    [ ] Short-term (1-3 months)
    [ ] Medium-term (3-6 months)
    [ ] Long-term (6-12 months)
    [ ] Exploratory (no specific timeline)

---

Thanks for completing this! I'll review your responses before our call and come
prepared with specific ideas.

Looking forward to speaking with you on [DATE/TIME].

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

---

## Customization by Industry

### B2B SaaS Companies
Add after question 10:
```
10b. How do you currently onboard new customers? Walk us through the steps.
10c. How do you handle support tickets — what's the flow from customer request to resolution?
```

### E-commerce
Add after question 10:
```
10b. What happens between a customer placing an order and receiving it?
10c. How do you handle returns and refunds?
```

### Professional Services (Agencies, Consultants)
Add after question 10:
```
10b. Walk us through what happens when you win a new client — from signed contract to first deliverable.
10c. How do you currently track time and generate invoices?
```

### Healthcare / Regulated Industries
Add after question 13:
```
14. Are there compliance or regulatory requirements we should know about?
    (e.g., HIPAA, GDPR, industry-specific regulations)
    [Your answer]

15. Are there any tools or platforms you're required to use?
    [Your answer]
```

## Delivery Options

**Option 1: Email** — Copy the template into an email. Simple, zero friction.

**Option 2: Google Form** — Create a form from the template. Better for organized responses, auto-saves.

**Option 3: Typeform** — More polished UI. Good for clients who appreciate design. Conditional logic available.

**Option 4: Notion template** — If the client already uses Notion. Collaborative editing possible.

Recommendation: Start with email for the first few audits. Switch to a form tool when you're running multiple audits simultaneously and need organized data.
